\# TRANSITION STATUS STATEMENT

\## NEXUS Sovereign Foundation v1



\### Status

\### Transition Meaning

NEXUS Sovereign Foundation v1 is ready to exit sandbox correction/hardening mode and enter Controlled Phase 1 — Governed Operational Baseline Use.



\### What the Sandbox Established

The sandbox program established that the foundation is structurally coherent and governance-safe within bounded internal scope. Specifically, it demonstrated:



\- corrected source-precedence discipline

\- removal of state-drift contradictions in the sandbox model

\- Guardian use as manual internal preflight, advisory only

\- governed current-state summary generation from stronger sources

\- comparative snapshot capability

\- explicit reversibility depth for at least one core correction action

\- minimal structured security telemetry

\- telemetry ingestion into security review

\- bounded severity/tiering interpretation

\- repeated event review

\- bounded response playbook logic

\- bounded rolling trend interpretation



\### What Is Now Operationally Adopted

The following are adopted as active operational baseline for Controlled Phase 1:



\- stronger sources outrank interface and summary surfaces

\- architecture governs lab posture

\- inactive reference surfaces do not participate in truth flow

\- Guardian preflight is required for governance-sensitive actions

\- Guardian remains manual internal preflight and advisory only

\- bounded representation rules apply unless stronger provenance justifies firmer wording

\- decision and consolidation traceability remain required

\- corrected foundation baseline logic is active for controlled use



\### What Remains Maturing

The following remain active but intentionally partial and must continue hardening during controlled use:



\- telemetry depth

\- severity/tiering richness

\- response playbook coverage

\- trend confidence

\- reversibility coverage breadth

\- broader incident/event-set volume



\### What Remains Excluded

The following remain excluded from operational truth role unless explicitly re-admitted through governed review:



\- META\_CONTROL\_CENTER.md as a truth surface

\- legacy labs as active architecture

\- speculative dashboard/control-surface material

\- stale export artifacts as authoritative state sources



\### Boundary Note

\- doctrine promotion

\- canonical approval

\- production certification

\- automatic authority expansion



\### Current Transition Posture

Proceed to Controlled Phase 1 — Governed Operational Baseline Use under existing governance boundaries and continued maturity hardening.
