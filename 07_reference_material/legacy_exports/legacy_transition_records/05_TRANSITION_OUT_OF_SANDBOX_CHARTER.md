\# TRANSITION OUT OF SANDBOX CHARTER

\## NEXUS Sovereign Foundation v1



\### Status

Sandbox maturity track closed successfully at the internal working level.  

Transition authorized into Controlled Phase 1 — Governed Operational Baseline Use.



\### Objective

Consolidate the results of the completed sandbox maturity program into a single governed transition posture for bounded post-sandbox use.



\### Transition Meaning

NEXUS Sovereign Foundation v1 is exiting sandbox correction/hardening mode and entering a controlled operational phase where:

\- corrected baseline rules are active

\- sandbox-only elements remain separated

\- partial maturity areas are carried forward explicitly

\- governance boundaries remain intact



\---



\## 1. What the Sandbox Established



The sandbox program established that the foundation is structurally coherent and governance-safe within bounded internal scope. It demonstrated:



\- corrected source-precedence discipline

\- removal of state-drift contradictions in the sandbox model

\- Guardian use as manual internal preflight, advisory only

\- governed current-state summary generation from stronger sources

\- comparative snapshot capability

\- explicit reversibility depth for at least one core correction action

\- minimal structured security telemetry

\- telemetry ingestion into security review

\- bounded severity/tiering interpretation

\- repeated event review

\- bounded response playbook logic

\- bounded rolling trend interpretation



\---



\## 2. Adopted Baseline



The following are adopted as active operational baseline for Controlled Phase 1:



\- stronger sources outrank interface and summary surfaces

\- architecture governs lab posture

\- inactive reference surfaces do not participate in truth flow

\- Guardian preflight is required for governance-sensitive actions

\- Guardian remains manual internal preflight and advisory only

\- bounded representation rules apply unless stronger provenance justifies firmer wording

\- decision and consolidation traceability remain required

\- corrected foundation baseline logic is active for controlled use



\---



\## 3. Carry-Forward Maturity Areas



The following remain active but intentionally partial and must continue hardening during controlled use:



\- telemetry depth

\- severity/tiering richness

\- response playbook coverage

\- trend confidence

\- reversibility coverage breadth

\- broader incident/event-set volume

\- security review maturity



These areas are usable in bounded form but are not to be presented as fully mature.



\---



\## 4. Exclusions



The following remain excluded from operational truth role unless explicitly re-admitted through governed review:



\- `META\_CONTROL\_CENTER.md` as a truth surface

\- legacy labs as active architecture

\- speculative dashboard/control-surface material

\- stale export artifacts as authoritative state sources



Reviewable does not mean active.



\---



\## 5. Controlled Phase 1 Scope



Controlled Phase 1 includes:



\- governed current-state summaries

\- bounded state review

\- comparative snapshot use

\- decision and consolidation logging

\- bounded telemetry-informed review

\- bounded trend interpretation

\- continued maturity hardening through controlled use



Controlled Phase 1 excludes by default:



\- doctrine promotion by momentum

\- canonical rewrite without explicit governed review

\- lab reactivation without explicit governed review

\- broad runtime expansion

\- uncontrolled agent expansion

\- interface-led authority claims



\---



\## 6. Acceptance Posture



Controlled Phase 1 should be treated as healthy when:



\- stronger-source discipline continues to hold

\- Guardian preflight is used where required

\- bounded wording and representation discipline remain intact

\- excluded materials do not re-enter truth flow

\- maturity carry-forward areas continue improving without overclaiming

\- no unplanned architecture or authority drift is introduced



\---



\## 7. Boundary Note

Sandbox-validated does not imply real-world validated.



This transition charter does not constitute:

\- doctrine promotion

\- canonical approval

\- production certification

\- automatic authority expansion



It is a governed transition artifact for moving from sandbox maturity work into bounded operational use.
