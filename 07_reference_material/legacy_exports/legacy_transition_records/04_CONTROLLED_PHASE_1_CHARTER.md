\# CONTROLLED PHASE 1 CHARTER

\## Governed Operational Baseline Use



\### Status

Active post-sandbox controlled phase  

Internal governed use  

Not doctrine promotion  

Not production certification



\### Objective

Move NEXUS Sovereign Foundation v1 from sandbox-corrected and sandbox-validated status into bounded governed operational use without re-entering contradiction cleanup or speculative expansion mode.



\### Phase Meaning

Controlled Phase 1 is the first post-sandbox phase in which the corrected foundation is used as an active operational baseline under continued governance discipline and explicit maturity carry-forward.



\---



\## 1. Phase Goals



Controlled Phase 1 exists to:



\- use the corrected foundation in real but bounded work

\- preserve source-precedence discipline during use

\- require Guardian preflight before governance-sensitive actions

\- preserve bounded representation and wording discipline

\- continue hardening partial maturity areas through controlled operation

\- gather better operational evidence without overclaiming readiness



\---



\## 2. Active Baseline for This Phase



Controlled Phase 1 adopts the active operational baseline defined in the Operational Baseline Register, including:



\- stronger sources outrank interface and summary surfaces

\- architecture governs lab posture

\- inactive references stay out of truth flow

\- Guardian remains manual internal preflight and advisory only

\- bounded wording is required unless stronger provenance justifies firmer wording

\- snapshots remain observational only

\- decision and consolidation traceability remain required

\- corrected foundation logic is active for controlled use



\---



\## 3. Allowed Scope
- bounded telemetry-informed governance review
- bounded maturity hardening during operational use



The following are in scope during Controlled Phase 1:



\- governed current-state summaries

\- bounded state reviews

\- comparative snapshot use

\- decision and consolidation logging

\- bounded telemetry-informed security review

\- bounded trend interpretation

\- explicit reversibility framing where practical

\- continued maturity hardening through controlled use



\---



\## 4. Excluded Scope



The following are excluded by default during Controlled Phase 1:



\- doctrine promotion by momentum

\- canonical rewrite without explicit governed review

\- lab reactivation without explicit governed review

\- broad runtime expansion

\- uncontrolled agent expansion

\- interface-led authority claims

\- use of excluded reference surfaces as operational truth



\---



\## 5. Required Governance Discipline



During Controlled Phase 1, the following remain mandatory:



\- stronger-source precedence

\- Guardian preflight for governance-sensitive actions

\- bounded representation rules

\- explicit residual uncertainty where needed

\- decision traceability

\- consolidation logging when triggered

\- no silent re-entry of excluded materials into truth flow



\---



\## 6. Maturity Carry-Forward Posture



The following remain active but partial during Controlled Phase 1:



\- telemetry depth

\- severity/tiering richness

\- response playbook coverage

\- trend confidence

\- reversibility coverage breadth

\- incident/event-set breadth

\- security review maturity



These areas are usable now, but must continue hardening during controlled use.



\---



\## 7. Excluded Materials Posture



The following remain excluded from operational truth role unless explicitly re-admitted through governed review:



\- `META\_CONTROL\_CENTER.md` as truth surface

\- legacy labs as active architecture

\- speculative dashboard/control-surface artifacts

\- stale export artifacts as authoritative state sources



Reviewable does not mean active.



\---



\## 8. Acceptance Posture for This Phase



Controlled Phase 1 is considered healthy when:



\- stronger-source discipline continues to hold

\- Guardian gating is used where required

\- operational summaries remain bounded and traceable

\- excluded surfaces do not re-enter truth flow

\- maturity carry-forward areas continue improving without being overclaimed

\- new work does not trigger unplanned architecture or authority drift



\---



\## 9. Escalation Note



Controlled Phase 1 remains under normal governance boundaries.



Escalate beyond bounded controlled use if:

\- architecture truth must change

\- protocol meaning must change

\- authority semantics must change

\- excluded materials need re-admission

\- lab posture must be altered rather than merely summarized



\---



\## 10. Boundary Note



This charter does not constitute:

\- doctrine promotion

\- production authorization

\- canonical completeness claim

\- automatic authority expansion



It defines the bounded operational posture for the first post-sandbox governed phase.
